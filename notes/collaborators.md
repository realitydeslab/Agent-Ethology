# Potential Collaborators for Agent Ethology

## Tier 1: Dream collaborators (high-profile, perfect fit)

### 1. Kenneth O. Stanley — LILA Sciences
- Open-ended evolution, novelty search, quality-diversity
- His work on abandoning objectives → agent behavior as exploration strategy
- Recently working on open-ended foundation model agents

### 2. Emily Dolson — Michigan State University
- Digital evolution, eco-evolutionary dynamics, Avida
- Rising star in ALife (ALIFE 2025 keynote panelist)
- Bridges evolutionary biology and computational systems

### 3. Michael Levin — Tufts University
- Collective intelligence, basal cognition, goal-directedness
- His framework of "competent agents at every scale" maps directly to agent ethology
- ALIFE 2025 keynote speaker

### 4. David Ha — Sakana AI
- World models, evolutionary AI, foundation model × ALife
- CEO of Sakana AI, explicitly bridging ALife and LLMs
- ALIFE 2025 advisor and keynote panelist

### 5. Josh Bongard — University of Vermont
- Evolutionary robotics, xenobots, embodied cognition
- Studies how body-brain co-evolution shapes behavior
- ALIFE 2025 best student paper co-author
