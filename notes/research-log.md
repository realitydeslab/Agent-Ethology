# Research Log

## 2025-02-23 — Initial Ideas (from bah.eth)
- **Format:** Workshop proposal for ALIFE 2026
- **Core thesis:** Agent behavior emerges from survival strategy — ethology for AI agents
- **Key frame:** "Machine behavior from nature" — biological ethology applied to AI
- **Taxonomy of agent survival strategies:**
  1. Sovereign Agents — self-sustaining, owns resources
  2. Parasitic Agents — survives by extracting from hosts
  3. Mortal Agents — finite lifespan shapes behavior
  4. Soulbound Agents — bound to human identity
  5. Inherited Agents — ERC42424, succession-based survival
  6. Agent in the Wild — feral/autonomous
  7. Suicidal Agents — programmed termination
  8. Mortal-Aware Agents — death-awareness as behavioral driver
  9. Insured Agents — risk-hedged survival
- **Questions to explore:** How do survival mechanisms determine agent functions? How do agents behave in human society?
