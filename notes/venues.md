# Venues for Agent Ethology — Ranked by Fit

## 1. ALIFE 2026 (★★★★★)
- **Dates:** Aug 17–21, 2026 | Waterloo, Canada
- **Workshop deadline:** Feb 28, 2026
- **Why:** Primary target. Core ALife audience understands emergence, evolution, open-endedness. Workshop format perfect for launching a new research direction.
- **Action:** Submit workshop proposal by Feb 28.

## 2. ECAL 2027 (★★★★★)
- **Dates:** TBD (biennial, likely summer 2027)
- **Why:** European ALife community, overlapping but distinct audience. Good for second edition if ALIFE 2026 succeeds.

## 3. GECCO 2026 (★★★★☆)
- **Dates:** Likely July 2026
- **Why:** Strong evolutionary computation track. "Digital Organisms" and "Artificial Life" are standing tracks. Large audience (~1500 attendees).
- **Caveat:** More engineering-focused, less philosophical.

## 4. AAMAS 2026 (★★★★☆)
- **Dates:** Likely May-July 2026
- **Why:** Premier multi-agent systems venue. Would reach AI agent researchers who aren't in ALife community.
- **Caveat:** Different framing needed — less "ethology," more "emergent agent behavior."

## 5. NeurIPS 2026 Workshop Track (★★★★☆)
- **Dates:** Dec 2026 | Deadline ~Sep 2026
- **Why:** Massive ML audience, high visibility. LLM agent researchers attend.
- **Caveat:** Very competitive workshop slots. Need strong ML framing.

## 6. ISAL Summer School (★★★☆☆)
- **Why:** Tutorial/course format. Good for establishing the field as teachable.

## 7. Santa Fe Institute Workshop (★★★☆☆)
- **Why:** Interdisciplinary complexity audience. Small, high-impact.
- **Format:** By invitation, need SFI connections.

## 8. ICML 2026 Workshop Track (★★★☆☆)
- **Similar to NeurIPS** but slightly smaller. Good alternative if NeurIPS too competitive.

## 9. CogSci 2026 (★★☆☆☆)
- **Why:** Cognitive science angle — ethology ↔ cognition. Niche but interesting crossover.

## 10. Animal Behavior Society Annual Meeting (★★☆☆☆)
- **Why:** Wild card. Presenting AI agent ethology to actual ethologists could spark unique collaborations.
- **Caveat:** Very different audience, would need careful framing.

---

**Recommended strategy:** ALIFE 2026 workshop (primary) → expand to GECCO or NeurIPS workshop later in 2026 → full paper submissions to ALIFE/ECAL 2027.
